Activities

Department automation

Thanks to qualified staff and state-of-the-art equipment implementing the most advanced techniques, this department has specialized in the design and installation of industrial automation and production line supervision systems. Its study service is perfectly capable of determining the technology to apply to automate your equipment.

Activities

  • Search for suitable technical solutions
  • Complete renovation
  • General installation studies: Functional analysis and programming
  • Design, conception, and wiring of electrical cabinets
  • Synoptic diagram creation
  • Commissioning
  • Study, development, supply, and commissioning of supervision and control/command tools
  • Technical assistance
  • Automation personnel training
  • Maintenance

Department industrial wiring


Design, wiring, and assembly of industrial electrical cabinets, individually or in series, according to your specifications in terms of finish and standards.

Activités

  • Control cabinets
  • Regulation cabinets
  • Automation cabinets
  • Distribution cabinets
  • Control panels with or without graphical synoptic
  • Possibility of study, sizing, and design of any type of electrical enclosures
  • Design and provision of technical documentation according to customer requests

Department industrial drilling & dosage

The department offers you a complete range of weighing and electrical dosing instruments selected from the best global brands, namely: precision balances, counting scales, industrial scales, weighbridges, weighing indicators, weighing conveyor belts, and all types of weight captures.

The main activities of this department include studying your weighing and dosing needs, providing and installing the most reliable and cost-effective solutions. Thanks to our highly skilled personnel and our fast and efficient service, our company has built a loyal clientele, and we invite you to be a part of it.
